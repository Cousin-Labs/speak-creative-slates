June creative bets — goal: higher P2 win rate via install-to-trial

Expanding the foundation of high-conviction iterative work while pushing more derivative concepts to build format diversity in channel. ITR is the goal KPI — the bets below are structured to create more at-bats at P2 with higher starting confidence.

Two priorities drive the June slate: expand the P1 Direct winner pool — the current ceiling on P2 win rate — and invent within proven P2 structures to compound what's already working.

June priority
Build P1 Direct winners
This is the June priority. Current P2 win rate is constrained by the size of the P1 winner pool. Functional hooks, direct outcome framing, Before & After, and expert voice are the entry points. These need P1 graduates in June to give Q3 anything new to iterate on.
Lead with Embed P2
The majority of iterative concepts run social proof or reaction moments before product reveal — the mechanic most correlated with high ITR in existing BAU winners.
Tighten proven P2 structures, don't rebuild them
Salon Surprise, She Was Shook, Telenovela, and Taco Bell have ITR data to beat; v2.0 sharpens the hook and proof sequence, not the format.
Expand derivatives to confirm format is the variable
Kitchen POV and No-Sabo Classroom move into new scenarios to test whether the format converts independent of the original moment.
Cast new creators into proven slots
Catherine and Gavriel slot into established concept structures so creator fit is the only unknown.
Use recuts as low-cost P2 probes
Remix top BAU assets to test whether combined proof moments outperform individual cuts — no new production spend required.
