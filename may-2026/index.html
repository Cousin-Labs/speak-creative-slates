May creative bets — retrospective read
  • IRL Spanish Demo is the conversion engine — all 3 P2 winners are IRL demo format (Ryanne listicle, Sofi nail salon, Aydreon poolside). The format dominates conversion while every other format is still searching for a P2 path.
  • Recuts efficient but ceiling is low — 29 recuts delivered, only CL-5-788 and CL-5-780 reached Scaling OK; none converted to P2 winner. Recuts are a P1 volume lever, not a P2 engine. CL-5-795 (Sofi ES-start recut) is the exception — it's a structural edit that changes the product pattern, not a trim.
  • Sofi May 8 batch was over-indexed — 14 assets from Sofi in a single week, mostly chisme and viral trend iterations. No P2 results from that group. The chisme concept has not transferred outside the Uber captive-audience context.
  • Word Wall format failed cleanly — 9 word wall assets in May 22–29. All P1 fails or early fails. SP scores cluster at 1.3–3.6, well below the 6+ threshold of the P2 winners. Format retirement confirmed.
  • Ryanne listicle is the net-new format find — CL-5-794 was the highest-ITR P2 winner (CTI 53%, CPFT $26.64). First listicle format to reach scaling winner status. No rationale on hooks in tracker — worth pulling the brief for July iteration.
  • Aydreon poolside Dominican dialect specificity worked — dialect specificity pre-selects higher-intent viewers. The specificity that narrows hook rate (40.2%) strengthens CTI (26.6% vs 19.3% barside). P1 SP of 6.46 was the second-highest of any May Cousins asset.
  • CL-5-940 (organic pulldown recut) highest SP in May — SP 7.16, highest P1 score of any Cousins May asset. Source footage was organic; recut for paid. The strongest P1 in May came from footage that wasn't produced under a paid brief — organic-pull content outperformed every scripted asset in the batch.
